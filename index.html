Description

Welcome to Utopia! You were just named mayor and got a big load of responsibilities. What makes it even harder, is that your town is heavily polluted! You now have to improve your town and create a perfect Utopia! To achieve this goal, you will have to play mini-games and gain points. We wish you good luck and an awesome adventure!

History

Early history

Dafel was created in the context of a schoolproject. We are a group of 4 students: Daphne Mayor, Andrea Christensen, Fabien Schanet and Lucy Munster

After that

Dafel wanted to publish their first game Utopia, to reach a wider public!
